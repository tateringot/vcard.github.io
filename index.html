Hello, my name is Matthew Rushing. I was born in New Hampshire but have lived the majority of my life in Greenville, South Carolina. I am currently a student at Bob Jones University and I am going into my senior year of studying graphic design. Lord willing, I plan to graduate in May of 2021 and persue a career in graphic design.


I have a wide range of interests in the fields of art and graphic design. You can check out my Instagram to see my photography and handlettering work. For my other work as well as an example of my web design, you can go to my online portfolio.I am also interested in the fields of animation and illustration.


I have done graphic design work for a few actual clients. I designed a website for my mom called Making Melody that allows people to play and download hymns she has written. I also designed the poster and various other applications including banners and backdrops for Bob Jones University's performance of Cosi Fan Tutte.
